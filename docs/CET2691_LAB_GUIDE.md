## Lab 1 - Homelab Rules of Engagement Document

### What You Will Do
Write a formal Rules of Engagement (ROE) document for all Matrix homelab engagements. Define authorized targets, attack windows, scope limitations, and emergency stop procedures.

ROE sections to cover:
- Authorized targets (Trinity, Sentinel, Architect only)
- Authorized techniques (Atomic Red Team, OpenVAS, DVWA)
- Out of scope (home network devices, SPC network, any third-party systems)
- Authorization statement (self-authorized lab environment)
- Emergency stop procedure
- Data handling (no real credentials, no PII in lab)
- Engagement window (defined time blocks)

### Skills Learned
- Rules of engagement documentation
- Scope definition and authorization frameworks
- Legal authorization for security testing
- Professional engagement documentation

### Purple Team Relevance
Every professional purple team engagement starts with a signed ROE. The ROE document shows employers you understand the legal boundary between authorized testing and unauthorized access.
